Important notes before vaccination

Please read carefully to ensure a safe and effective visit for your pet.

  • 01
    Your pet must be completely healthy

    Do not vaccinate if your pet has a fever, diarrhoea, vomiting or any sign of illness. Please inform staff if your pet is currently on medication.

  • 02
    Old enough to vaccinate

    Dogs and cats must be at least 6вҖ“8 weeks old before their first vaccination. Do not vaccinate pregnant or lactating animals.

  • 03
    Bring the previous vaccination record

    If your pet has been vaccinated before, please bring their booklet or card so we can review history and avoid duplicate doses.

  • 04
    Feed lightly before the visit

    Give a light meal (not too full) 2вҖ“3 hours before the appointment. This helps reduce nausea and anxiety during vaccination.

  • 05
    Monitor for at least 30 minutes post-vaccination

    Allergic reactions, if any, typically appear within the first 30 minutes. Your pet will be observed at the clinic before leaving.

  • 06
    No bathing within 24 hours of vaccination

    Avoid getting your pet wet, cold or overexerted on the day of vaccination to allow the vaccine to take full effect.
