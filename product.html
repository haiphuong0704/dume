About this product

Our Organic Premium Dog Food is the result of three years of veterinary research and collaboration with certified animal nutritionists. Every ingredient is sourced from verified organic farms and cold-pressed to preserve natural enzymes, antioxidants and essential fatty acids.

The formula is built around a single premium protein source — free-range chicken — combined with organic sweet potato, brown rice and a proprietary blend of herbs and superfoods. No soy, wheat, corn or artificial colours.

Key benefits

  • Supports healthy digestion with prebiotic fibre from chicory root
  • Maintains coat and skin health through omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids
  • Builds lean muscle mass with 30% premium protein content
  • Supports joint health with natural glucosamine from chicken cartilage
  • Calming formula with chamomile and valerian root for anxious dogs

Full ingredient list

Free-range chicken (42%), organic sweet potato (18%), brown rice (12%), chicken fat (preserved with vitamin E), organic peas, organic carrots, organic flaxseed, salmon oil, chicory root extract (fructooligosaccharides), dried chamomile, dried valerian root, vitamin and mineral premix.
