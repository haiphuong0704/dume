What's included in a wellness plan?

Each plan includes scheduled health exams, vet consultations, vaccination reviews, dental screenings, and access to our specialist team. Premium and Elite plans also include nutrition consultations, priority booking, and 24/7 phone support.

How is a wellness plan different from pet insurance?

Pet insurance covers unexpected illnesses and accidents — it's reactive. A wellness plan is proactive. It covers routine and preventive care (checkups, vaccinations, nutrition) designed to keep your pet healthy so emergencies are less likely in the first place. The two work well together.

Can I upgrade or change my plan?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or pause your plan at any time. Changes take effect from the next billing cycle. There are no fees for changing plans.

How often are health reviews scheduled?

Essential plan members receive an annual comprehensive exam. Premium members receive quarterly reviews (4 per year). Elite members receive monthly check-ins with a dedicated wellness specialist.

Are wellness plans suitable for senior pets?

Absolutely. Senior pets (7+ years for dogs, 10+ for cats) benefit most from regular monitoring. We offer age-adjusted care protocols and recommend our Premium or Elite plans for older animals where early detection is especially important.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel anytime by contacting our team at least 5 days before your next billing date. There are no cancellation fees and you keep all benefits until the end of your current period.
