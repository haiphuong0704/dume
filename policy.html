01

Overview

A summary of all our policies in one place.

At Petcare Spa, we hold ourselves to high standards of transparency, care, and accountability. These policies apply to all products purchased through our website and all services booked at our locations.

We regularly review and update our policies to reflect the evolving needs of our community. If you have any questions that aren't answered here, our support team is always available to help.

Fast Shipping

2–5 business day delivery on most orders, with express options available.

Easy Returns

30-day hassle-free return window for unopened products in original packaging.

Data Privacy

Your personal information is never sold. We use industry-standard security throughout.

02

Shipping Policy

How and when we deliver your order.

Processing Times

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of placement. Orders placed after 2pm on Fridays, or on weekends and public holidays, will be processed the next business day.

We currently ship to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Delivery times for international orders range from 7–14 business days.

03

Refunds & Returns

Our commitment to your satisfaction.

Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date. To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as received.

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Opened food or supplement products (for hygiene and safety reasons)
  • Personalised or custom-made items
  • Items marked as final sale at the time of purchase
  • Digital downloads or e-gift cards

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at returns@petcarespa.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Our team will respond within 1–2 business days with a prepaid return label.
  3. Package the item securely and drop it off at any authorised shipping location.
  4. Once we receive and inspect the return, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Service appointments cancelled more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund or complimentary rebooking at no charge.

04

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, place an order, or book an appointment, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details. We may also collect usage data to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfil your orders and bookings
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, tracking updates)
  • Improve our website and personalise your experience
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent, and you may unsubscribe at any time)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., payment processors, shipping carriers), under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To submit a request, contact our privacy team at privacy@petcarespa.com.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep our site functioning and optional analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our platform. You can manage cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.

05

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common policy questions.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes. If you'd like a different size, colour, or product, contact our team within 30 days and we'll arrange an exchange. If there's a price difference, we'll either charge or refund the balance accordingly.

What if my order arrives damaged?

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please photograph the packaging and item immediately and email us at support@petcarespa.com. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking link by email as soon as your order is dispatched. You can also log into your account to view your order status at any time.

Do you offer price matching?

We do not currently offer price matching. However, we regularly run promotions and loyalty rewards for members. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know about new offers.

How is my payment information secured?

All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment providers. We never store your full card details on our servers. All data transmissions are encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.

Still have questions?

We're here to help.

Our support team typically responds within a few hours on business days. We're always happy to clarify anything about our policies or your order.
