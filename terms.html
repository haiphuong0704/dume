Bookings & Appointments

Making a Booking

Appointments can be made through our website, by phone, or in person at any Petcare Spa location. All bookings are subject to availability and are confirmed only upon receipt of a confirmation email from us.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both you and our team:

24+ hours notice

Full refund or free rescheduling, no questions asked.

Less than 24 hours

50% service charge applies. The balance can be applied as a credit.

No-show

Full service fee charged. Three no-shows may result in booking restrictions.

Late Arrivals

We allow a 10-minute grace period for late arrivals. If you arrive more than 10 minutes late, we may need to shorten your appointment or reschedule, depending on our schedule for that day. The full appointment fee will still apply.

Emergency Cancellations

We understand that true emergencies happen. Cancellations due to documented medical emergencies or severe weather events will be accommodated with a full refund or free reschedule, regardless of notice period.
