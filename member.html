Questions

Frequently asked.

Everything you need to know before you join.

How does my membership work?

Once you join, your plan activates immediately. You’ll receive a welcome email with your member card, plan details, and instructions to book your first appointment. All benefits are available from day one — priority booking, discounts, consultation support, and Spa Bites delivery.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime from your member dashboard or by contacting our team. Your benefits remain active through the end of your current billing period, and there are absolutely no cancellation fees.

How do loyalty points work?

You earn 1 loyalty point for every 10.000₫ spent on services, grooming, Spa Bites, and shop purchases. Points accumulate in your account and can be redeemed for free services, grooming sessions, or Spa Bites credits. Points never expire while your membership is active.

Where does Spa Bites delivery cover?

We currently deliver within a 30-km radius of our main locations. Members receive free refrigerated delivery on all Spa Bites orders regardless of size. We are expanding our coverage area regularly — contact us to check availability in your neighborhood.

How do I book appointments as a member?

Members book via our website booking page or by calling our priority line. Your member status is recognized automatically. Essential members receive priority in the standard queue; Premium and Elite members access dedicated specialist slots, often same-day or next-morning.
